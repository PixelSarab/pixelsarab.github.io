I’m Neil Messelmani, I am Czech-Tunisian with love for Development and experience in Project/Team Managment.

I have worked in a multinational corporation, Cimpress (previously Vistaprint) which employs over 10’000 employee globally and have a revenue of more than 2B $ so that implies that we worked cross time-zones, which was challenging. I started there as Production Designer, which consisted in localising assets. I took every new project opportunity, proving my versatility within the organization, which consisted on needs at the time with no individual to work on it, they were mostly in-house softwares which I need to learn and be profiscient at quickly, I pride myself on being a fast learner.
I helped with IT technicalities as well, which ranges from hardware setup to script development and software setup. I also led in engagement activities, like paper airplane origami challenge and miscelaneous games. In the end I opened the market for retouching on site, and finally expaned my team..
As a senior my responsibilities were in leadership, project management, and doing design work, which was mostly retouching. Even though I was a retoucher, I implemented development as often as I can. That meant scripting photoshop to automate the tedious export process, and a tool that fetches data from a database and creats photoshop documents based on the fetched data.
Also in cimpress there were a training for first aid which I took.
