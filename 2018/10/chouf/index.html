#Projects#Video-Editing
Oct 14, 2018

I worked with some cool people on a documentary about a couple where both of them are blind. I did the set the subtitles and co-worked on the video editing.
This documentary won multiple prizes and got featured in numerous places:

- 1st Prize in the French-German competition 2014
- Selected at JCC Tunisia 2014
- Prize for best documentary: "Les nuits du court-metrage tunisien" in Paris, France 2015
- Selected in AFLAM festival in Marseille, France 2015
- Select at the "Arab Short Film Festival" in Beirut, Lebanon 2015
- Screening at the "Festival Del Film LOCARNO-SUISSE" 2015
- Opened at the Festival of Hergla, in Hergla, Tunisia 2015
- festival international du film documentaire à REDEYEF-TUNISIE 2015,
- Best Arab short-film at the Festival of Alexandria, EGYPTE 2015
- Participation Malmo Arab Film Festival-MAFF, Sweden 2015
